Overview

PinPin Blocks is a free, ad-supported game. We do not require registration and we do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information ourselves. All game progress (scores, coins, skill inventory) is stored locally on your device only.

Advertising

This app displays ads served by Google AdMob (Google LLC). AdMob may collect and process certain data to serve and measure ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (e.g., Android Advertising ID)
  • Approximate location (derived from IP address)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)
  • General device information (model, OS version)

For details on how Google uses this data, see the Google Privacy Policy and How Google uses data from partner apps.

You can reset or delete your Advertising ID, or opt out of ad personalization, in your device settings (Settings → Google → Ads).

Children

This app is intended for a general audience and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Deletion

Uninstalling the app deletes all locally stored game data. We hold no server-side data about you.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted at this page with a new "Last updated" date.
